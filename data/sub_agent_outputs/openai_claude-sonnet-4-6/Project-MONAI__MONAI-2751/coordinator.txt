However, the agent's evidence shows this as a "distinct feature" presented alongside Path support in the commit message, suggesting it may have independent value beyond just Path support.

**Conclusion**: The evidence is AMBIGUOUS. The extension check could be interpreted as either (a) implementation detail of sanctioned IS expansion, or (b) genuinely additional scope.
